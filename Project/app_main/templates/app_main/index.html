Risk Disclosures
Introduction

We at Namaa are committed to transparency for both investors and projects. However, investing through the Namaa crowdfunding platform carries a number of risks that all parties should consider before making investment decisions.

Investment Risks
  • Capital Loss: There is a significant risk of losing part or all of the invested capital, as startups carry higher risks compared to large and stable companies.
  • Lack of Liquidity: Investments in startups are often illiquid, meaning you may not be able to sell or convert your investment into cash anytime soon.
  • Business Failure: The company may fail to achieve its objectives or commercial success, leading to a loss of investment.
Market Risks
  • Market Volatility: Changes in economic market conditions can negatively impact the company's performance and ability to generate profits.
  • Competition: The company may face intense competition from other firms, affecting its market share and profitability.
Legal Risks
  • Intellectual Property Issues: The company may face legal claims related to intellectual property rights, leading to high legal costs and negative impacts on the project.
Disclaimer

These disclosures do not cover all potential risks associated with investing through the Namaa crowdfunding platform. Investors should conduct their own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decision.
